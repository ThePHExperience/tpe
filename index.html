LUZON
 Luzon, largest and most important island in the Philippines. It is the site of Manila, the nation's capital and the major metropolis, and of Quezon City. Located on the northern part of the Philippine archipelago.

MINDANAO
 Being the second largest island group next to Luzon, Mindanao has been dubbed the country's "Land of Promise" becasue of its promising natural resources. It is also known worldwide for being the surf capital of the Philippines, a famous highly-urbanized city, and jaw-dropping beaches.
