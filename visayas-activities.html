CANYONEERING

Cebu, Philippines

Try Canyoneering in Cebu
For adrenaline junkies who want to try something different, canyoneering or canyoning will surely make waves. If you’re looking for an extra dose of outdoor fun and adventure where you get to enjoy the natural scenery and test your limits, then you must try going on a canyoneering tour.

Experience Canyoneering in Kawasan Falls, where you can swim in clear turquoise water surrounded by lush green forests with the noise of powerful waterfalls rushing in the background.

WATERSPORTS

Tacloban, Leyte, Philippines

Watersports in Tacloban City
Summer is a beautiful season — it’s full of long days, warm nights, and fragrant air. Though, of course, it does bring with it a lot more heat and sweaty days. That’s why joining in on water sports or activities is the perfect way to cool off, freshen up, and have a whole lot of fun in the meantime.

If you are looking for the best water ports and activities for your summer inspiration, visit Aqua Momentum in Tacloban City.

HOT AIR
BALLOON

Bohol, Philippines

Ride Hot air balloon in the Chocolate Hills of Bohol
Soar over the beautiful Bohol countryside with a scenic hot air balloon ride. Get an early morning start and take advantage of convenient shuttle transportation from your hotel. Climb aboard your colorful hot air balloon and take flight. Watch a spectacular sunrise, peek through the famous Chocolate Hills, and see the hills turn into a warm, chocolate brown.

Glide over sprawling landscapes and lush fields as you bask in the morning sunlight. Capture the moment with the on-site Go-Pro camera. Glimpse incredible panoramic views of Bohol on this exciting hot air balloon ride.

BAMBOO
RAFT

Cebu, Philippines

Ride a bamboo raft at Kawasan Falls
Situated in Badian Cebu is one of the most famous falls in the Visayas region, Kawasan Falls. The water here cascades into a beautiful turquoise pool of water, perfect for swimming. The surrounding greenery also makes for a picture-perfect backdrop. What makes Kawasan Falls even more exciting is their bamboo raft activity.

For a nominal fee, a tour guide will paddle you out on a bamboo raft toward the waterfall. Once you are under the fall, the pressure of the water makes for the perfect natural back massage.

WHALE
SHARKS

Oslob, Cebu, Philippines

Swim with Whale Sharks in Cebu City
Swimming with Whale Sharks is one of the most fascinating attractions in the Visayas. They reach up to 15 meters in length, have flat heads, wide mouths and long tails. You can swim with these beautiful giants in an area called Oslob – just a few hours south of Cebu City.

While the words Whale and Shark may send some people running for the hills, ‘Whale Sharks” is actually the world’s biggest fish and eat only krill (or small shrimp). In other words, people are friends, not food.

DIVING

Malapascua Island, Cebu, Philippines

Explore the Underwater Life at Malapascua Island
The huge diversity of marine life near Malapascua Island is what draws many divers in from around the world. While the list of wonderful creatures here is long, the area is most famous for its daily Thresher Shark sightings. The Thresher Shark is distinctive for its long tail that resembles a scythe.

If diving into deep waters is not your cup of tea, you can also find great snorkeling! Here you can observe various forms of marine life, caves, and even shipwrecks!

SEA TURTLES

Apo Island, Negros Oriental, Philippines

Swim with Sea Turtles near Apo Island
While Apo Island is known for being a premier snorkeling and diving site, it’s also a one-of-a-kind destination for a reason. Near the shores of the Island and in shallow waters are an abundance of sea turtles! That’s right, there is no need to swim far to see them. Apo Islands Sea Turtles swim close to shore.

Everyone’s main reason for visiting Apo Island is for the world-famous snorkelling and diving. So, equipment is readily on hand for hire. It’s a relatively pretty island, with one small beach and beautiful rock formations but of course it’s simply the marine life that draws tourists to Apo Island.

AERIAL
TOUR

Dumaguete, Philippines

30 Minute Scenic Aerial Tour of Dumaguete and Nearby Islands
Enjoy aerial views of the Island of Apo in Dauin, Negros Oriental on a 30-minute scenic flight from Dumaguete City. With window seats guaranteed, this flight features informative commentary from your pilot as you fly over the city of Dumaguete, then head out over the turquoise sea to coastal line of Dumaguete and nearby islands.

You'll pass over Sumilon Island, Cebu, Siquijor, and the scenic view of the entire mountain ranges of Orientals before heading back to the mainland.
